Beyond a Century of Endeavour

Rev. Fr. Prof. Lawrence Njoroge, a Professor in the College of Human Resource and Development (COHRED), JKUAT launched a book; "Beyond Century of Endeavor" on Saturday, 19th November 2022. The book is about the history of the Catholic Church from the times of Vasco Da Gama to the year of 2022, a first of its kind in Kenya.
