INSIDE CATCOM

How we operate

Together, we create harmonious melodies that uplift spirits and touch hearts. Our people, Our culture

Named after patron saints, Small Christian Communities(SCC's) are smaller closeknit families of the bigger CATCOM community.

Mary motherof God

The Mary, Mother of God SCC is a close-knit community of individuals who gather together to honor and emulate the virtues of the Blessed Virgin Mary. Just as Mary said "yes" to God's plan with unwavering faith and humility, our SCC seeks to deepen our relationship with God and follow in her footsteps. In our SCC, we find inspiration in Mary's devotion to her Son, Jesus, and her role as the Mother of God. We strive to cultivate a deep love for Jesus and a heartfelt devotion to Mary, recognizing

Through regular meetings, prayer gatherings, and Marian devotions, we come together to express our love for Mary and seek her guidance and intercession in our lives. We delve into the mysteries of the Rosary, meditating on the life of Jesus through Mary's eyes and finding solace in her motherly care and protection. Our SCC also emphasizes the importance of nurturing our relationships as a spiritual family. We support one another in our personal journeys of faith, offering a listening ear, words of encouragement, and a helping hand when needed. Together, we strive to grow in holiness, just as Mary did, by fostering virtues such as humility, compassion, and surrender to God's will. As members of the Mary, Mother of God SCC, we are not only devoted to our blessed Mother, but we also extend her love and compassion to others. We actively engage in acts of charity, reaching out to those in need and sharing the message of God's love and mercy with our community. We strive to emulate Mary's selfless love, seeking opportunities to serve and uplift those around us. Joining the Mary, Mother of God SCC is an invitation to deepen your relationship with Jesus and to embrace the tender love and guidance of the Blessed Virgin Mary. Together, let us grow in holiness, drawing strength from Mary's example, and allowing her to lead us closer to her Son, our Lord and Savior.

St. Chrales Lwanga

Named after the patron of youth and Caholic action in Africa (Luganda: Kaloli Lwanga, 1st Jan 1860-3rd June 1886), St. Charles lwanga SCC is an amazing family. Our SCC (Small Christian Community) is a place where individuals from all walks of life come together to deepen their relationship with God and support one another on their faith journeys. Just as St. Charles Lwanga stood up for his beliefs, we strive to uphold his values of courage, integrity, and unwavering commitment to Christ.

At St. Charles Lwanga SCC, we offer a range of activities and opportunities for spiritual enrichment. Through prayer, scripture study, fellowship, and service projects, we foster a sense of community and actively live out our faith in daily life. We aim to empower individuals to become active participants in their faith, encouraging them to apply Catholic principles to their personal lives and make a positive impact in their families, workplaces, and communities. Our SCC is a place of belonging, where individuals of all ages can find support, guidance, and friendship. Whether you are a young person seeking to deepen your faith, a parent looking for spiritual resources, or a member of the community interested in engaging with like-minded individuals, St. Charles Lwanga SCC welcomes you with open arms.

St. Jude

Named after the patron saint of desperate and hopeless cases, the St. Jude SCC is a compassionate and supportive community that gathers together to seek intercession and find hope in challenging times. Inspired by the life and example of St. Jude, known for his unwavering faith and his willingness to intercede for those in need, our SCC aims to provide a haven of hope and healing for individuals facing difficult situations. We understand that life can present

us with seemingly insurmountable obstacles, and it is during these moments that the intercession of St. Jude can bring solace and strength. In our SCC, we come together to share our stories, offer support, and pray for one another. We create a safe and non-judgmental space where individuals can openly express their struggles, knowing that they will be met with empathy and understanding. Through prayer meetings, group discussions, and spiritual reflections, we seek to deepen our trust in God's providence and the intercession of St. Jude. Just as St. Jude tirelessly advocated for those in desperate situations, our SCC extends its outreach to those in need within our community. We actively engage in acts of charity and service, extending a helping hand to the marginalized, the vulnerable, and the forgotten. By emulating St. Jude's selflessness, we strive to be a source of hope and support to those who may feel lost or abandoned. Within the St. Jude SCC, we believe that no challenge is too great when approached with faith, community, and the intercession of our patron saint. We draw strength from St. Jude's unwavering belief in the power of God's love and mercy, as we navigate through life's trials together. If you are facing desperate circumstances or simply seek a community that understands and supports you, we invite you to join us at the St. Jude SCC. Together, let us lean on the intercession of St. Jude and discover the hope and resilience that faith can bring, even in the midst of our darkest moments.

St. Martin

The St. Martin SCC is a vibrant and compassionate community named after the patron saint of social justice and charitable works. Inspired by the life and teachings of St. Martin of Tours, we come together to actively live out our faith by serving those in need and fostering a spirit of generosity and inclusivity. In our SCC, we strive to emulate St. Martin's spirit of selflessness and his deep commitment to helping others. We believe that our faith should be reflected in our actions and that true discipleship involves reaching out to those who are marginalized, oppressed, or suffering. Through regular gatherings, discussions, and service projects, we deepen our understanding of social justice issues and explore practical ways to make a positive impact in our community. We collaborate with local organizations, participate in outreach programs, and organize initiatives to address issues such as poverty, inequality, and discrimination. The St. Martin SCC also serves as a place of refuge and support for individuals who are seeking solace, healing, or guidance. We foster an atmosphere of acceptance and empathy, where everyone is welcomed, valued, and embraced. Our SCC members offer listening ears, compassionate hearts, and a network of support to those facing personal challenges, providing a sense of belonging and encouragement along their spiritual journey. As a community dedicated to St. Martin's legacy, we recognize the importance of personal and spiritual growth. We engage in regular prayer, reflection, and Bible study to deepen our relationship with God and to gain wisdom from His word. We encourage one another to cultivate virtues such as humility, compassion, and integrity, following in the footsteps of our patron saint. The St. Martin SCC invites individuals who are passionate about social justice, charitable works, and building a more inclusive society to join us. Together, let us live out our faith in tangible ways, inspiring others through acts of service and fostering a community where love, justice, and compassion are at the core of all we do.

St. Stephen

The St. Stephen SCC is a community named after the first Christian martyr and deacon, known for his unwavering faith,courage, and dedication to serving others. Inspired by St. Stephen's example, we gather together to deepen our faith, grow in holiness, and actively engage in acts of service and evangelization.

In our SCC, we seek to embody the spirit of St. Stephen by fostering a strong sense of community and unity. We come together to worship, pray, and support one another in our spiritual journeys. Through regular meetings, fellowship gatherings, and faith-sharing sessions, we create an environment where individuals can deepen their understanding of the Gospel and develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. Following St. Stephen's example of service, we actively engage in outreach activities to extend God's love and compassion to those in need. Whether it's through charitable works, volunteering at local organizations, or providing assistance to marginalized communities, our SCC strives to be a visible sign of Christ's presence in the world. St. Stephen also serves as an inspiration for us to boldly proclaim and defend our faith. We encourage members to actively participate in evangelization efforts, sharing the good news of Jesus Christ with others through both words and actions. We seek to equip one another with the knowledge and confidence to articulate and defend our beliefs, promoting dialogue and understanding in our communities. As a community named after St. Stephen, we also embrace the values of forgiveness, humility, and reconciliation. We recognize the importance of seeking healing and unity, both within our SCC and in the wider world. We encourage open dialogue, mutual respect, and a spirit of forgiveness, as we strive to build bridges and mend broken relationships. Joining the St. Stephen SCC means becoming part of a community that is committed to growing in faith, serving others, and fearlessly proclaiming the Gospel. Together, let us draw inspiration from St. Stephen's life and martyrdom, embracing his virtues of faith, service, and courage, as we strive to make a positive impact in our world.

St. Therese

The St. Therese SCC is a community named after the beloved patroness of missionaries and known for her "Little Way" of spiritual simplicity and love. Inspired by the life and teachings of St. Therese of Lisieux, we come together to foster a deep and personal relationship with God, embracing a spirituality of childlike trust and self-giving love.

In our SCC, we seek to emulate St. Therese's "Little Way" by finding joy and holiness in the ordinary moments of life. We believe that even the smallest acts of love and kindness have the power to transform the world. Through regular meetings, prayer gatherings, and reflections on St. Therese's writings, we strive to deepen our understanding of her spirituality and apply it to our daily lives. The St. Therese SCC provides a nurturing and supportive environment where individuals can share their spiritual journeys and encourage one another in their faith. We emphasize the importance of simplicity, humility, and trust in God's providence. Together, we learn to surrender our worries, doubts, and insecurities to God, relying on His mercy and love to guide us along the path of holiness. St. Therese's devotion to the mission of the Church also inspires us to be active participants in spreading the love of Christ. As a community, we engage in acts of service and evangelization, seeking to bring God's light and compassion to those around us. We support missionary endeavors, participate in outreach programs, and strive to be instruments of God's healing and reconciliation in the world. Within the St. Therese SCC, we also cultivate a spirit of prayer and contemplation. We encourage members to deepen their personal prayer lives, fostering an intimate connection with God. Through quiet reflection, meditation, and devotion, we seek to grow in our love for God and to listen attentively to His gentle whispers in our hearts. If you are seeking a community that embraces simplicity, love, and a childlike trust in God, we invite you to join us at the St. Therese SCC. Together, let us learn from St. Therese's example of joyful surrender to God's will, and through our little acts of love, may we become channels of God's grace and bringers of hope in our world.

St. Veronica

Named after the patron saint of compassion and mercy, the St. Veronica SCC is a community that embraces the spirit of selflessness and empathy. Inspired by the story of St. Veronica's act of kindness towards Jesus on His way to Calvary, we come together to deepen our understanding of compassion and to extend that compassion to others.

In the St. Veronica SCC, we seek to embody the virtues of love, mercy, and solidarity. Just as St. Veronica showed compassion by wiping the face of Jesus, we strive to recognize the face of Christ in those we encounter in our daily lives. Through regular gatherings, prayer sessions, and reflections on St. Veronica's example, we aim to develop a greater sensitivity to the needs of others and to respond with acts of love and kindness. Our SCC fosters a welcoming and inclusive environment where individuals can share their joys, sorrows, and struggles. We provide a safe space for open dialogue, mutual support, and encouragement, recognizing that each person's journey is unique and valuable. Through our collective experiences and shared faith, we strive to uplift and accompany one another on the path of discipleship. As members of the St. Veronica SCC, we actively engage in works of mercy and charitable endeavors. We reach out to the marginalized, the lonely, and the suffering, offering practical assistance, a listening ear, and a compassionate heart. We collaborate with local organizations and actively participate in initiatives that promote justice, fairness, and dignity for all. Within our SCC, we also recognize the importance of personal growth and spiritual nourishment. We encourage individuals to deepen their relationship with God through prayer, Scripture study, and the sacraments. By drawing inspiration from St. Veronica's courage and selflessness, we strive to cultivate a greater capacity for love and compassion in our own lives. If you are seeking a community that embraces the values of compassion, mercy, and solidarity, we invite you to join us at the St. Veronica SCC. Together, let us follow in the footsteps of our patron saint, extending the love of Christ to all, and making a positive difference in the lives of those we encounter.

St. Paul

The St. Paul SCC is a vibrant community named after the great apostle and missionary, known for his zeal, intellect, and unwavering dedication to spreading the Gospel. Inspired by the life and teachings of St. Paul, we come together to deepen our faith, grow in knowledge and understanding of God's Word, and actively

Through regular meetings, prayer gatherings, and Bible study sessions, we explore the richness of Scripture and seek to apply its timeless teachings to our lives. We encourage open dialogue and thoughtful discussions, allowing for personal growth and a deeper connection to our faith. The St. Paul SCC places a strong emphasis on evangelization and discipleship. We recognize that each member is called to be a witness to the Gospel in their own unique way. Through intentional outreach efforts, mentorship programs, and supportive relationships, we aim to equip and empower one another to boldly proclaim our faith and make a positive impact in the world around us. Just as St. Paul's conversion experience transformed his life, we also recognize the power of personal transformation through encountering Christ. We encourage individuals to deepen their personal relationship with Jesus, nurturing a vibrant prayer life and seeking the guidance of the Holy Spirit. We embrace the diversity of spiritual gifts within our SCC, fostering an environment where everyone is encouraged to use their talents and abilities to serve God and others. As members of the St. Paul SCC, we are not only committed to personal growth and evangelization but also to building a strong sense of community. We support one another through times of joy and difficulty, offering encouragement, accountability, and friendship. We strive to foster an environment of mutual respect, acceptance, and genuine love, reflecting the unity and diversity of the Body of Christ. If you are seeking a community that is passionate about growing in faith, sharing the Gospel, and building authentic relationships, we invite you to join us at the St. Paul SCC. Together, let us follow in the footsteps of our patron saint, embracing the call to be bold and unyielding in our faith, as we strive to bring the light of Christ to the world around us.

Kwaya ya Mtunzi Romano

Our story
Initially JKUAT Catcom Choir, KMRM was renamed in 2022 after the patron of music: Saint Romanos the Melodist. The choir manages a YouTube channel where they upload song albums with sixth one was released 2020 and launched February 2021. The choir also offer music classes to the choir members and other interested persons at a fee.
The choir is building the KMRM Studio which will not only serve the choir but the catholic community, the St. Augustine Parish and other interested parties for audio and video productions. The family of approximately 100 makes every second mass at St. Augustine Centre lively as ever.We meet at 3.00pm of every friday and saturday for a 2 hour practice session.
