Become an alumni

Why join us

Joining our alumni community at CATCOM is an opportunity to reconnect with your faith, your fellow alumni, and the values that have shaped your journey. As an alumni member, you'll have the chance to relive the cherished memories of your time with us and contribute to the growth of our vibrant community. But it's not just about nostalgia; it's about staying connected, sharing experiences, and supporting each other in your ongoing journeys. By registering as an alumni, you'll be part of a dedicated and welcoming community that is committed to helping you continue to be the best version of yourself. Join us today and be an integral part of the CATCOM legacy!
